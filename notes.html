Things I Think About

If we treat biology like a vast optimization experiment that has been running for hundreds of millions of years, then each experiment asks "what constraint was evolution solving?" The natural follow up to ask is whether AI can benefit from solving that constraint in the same way. These notes are a collection of my thoughts on this research philosophy over the years.

  • Human brains are mostly just predicting the next token, too

  • The most sophisticated brain on Earth is optimized for cooperation. Maybe AI should be too?
