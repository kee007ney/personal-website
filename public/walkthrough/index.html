Game Walkthrough

The journey from simple chemistry to engineered life.

Atlas Vegrandis is an open-world survival craft game about building biological systems from the inside out. This page outlines the current progression path, major zones, core mechanics, and concept direction.

01 Starting Zone 02 Fungal Zone 03 High Oxygen Zone 04 The Chamber

You are an Endonaut.

You pilot an organic nanobot powered by ATP, the same molecular fuel that powers life. You scan, harvest, synthesize, and assemble living structures to survive environments that keep escalating in scale and complexity.

01

Starting Zone

Learn scanning, harvesting, ATP synthesis, vesicles, membranes, cell walls, channels, and early photosynthesis.

Core loop

  1. Scan the environment.
  2. Harvest simple molecular resources.
  3. Craft ATP, lipid, membrane, and vesicles.
  4. Build your first cellular structures.

Mini task

ATP

Find phosphorus, oxygen, ribose, and adenine to synthesize ATP manually.

~15–20 min.

Mini task

Endoplasmic Reticulum

Automate lipid and membrane production using scanned biological structure plans.

~20–25 min.

02

Fungal Zone

A low-oxygen biome rich in sugars, fungal structures, megafauna, antibiotics, and the transition toward eukaryotic complexity.

Major task

Eukaryogenesis

Build sturdier cellular systems: nucleus, rough ER, smooth ER, cytoskeleton, Golgi, lysosome, and photosynthetic support.

~3 hr.

Zone pressure

Basic cells deteriorate quickly here. The player needs internal organization and more robust cellular architecture.

03

High Oxygen Zone

A bright, plant-dominated biome where oxygen becomes dangerous, forcing antioxidant systems, DNA stability, and multicellular construction.

Mini task

Antioxidants

Scan and build superoxide dismutase to survive the oxidative environment.

~5 min.

Mini task

DNA

Stabilize RNA-like systems into DNA-like systems and automate protein production.

~30–60 min.

Major task

Pathogenesis

Use pathogenic bacteria and phage systems to break through blocked plant structures.

~1 hr.

04

The Chamber

The player must build a large differentiated multicellular structure connecting water and light while managing energy costs, pathogens, and cell specialization.

Major task

Mitosis

Duplicate cellular structures using DNA synthase, centrosomes, kinetochores, microtubules, and contractile rings.

~2–3 hr.

Major task

Differentiation

Create root-like and leaf-like cells using a pathway system that responds to environmental inputs.

~1–2 hr.

Sandbox

After completing the main progression, the Gene Builder opens. Players can craft custom genes, localization sequences, and large biological megastructures.

Arid Zone

A dangerous boundary biome surrounding the map, populated by large predators and resistant fauna.
